Procurement ka gyaanRazor Procurement process,

    * Every month, we do demand projection for next rolling year (State of the art ML model)
* PO creation, 
    * Based on our current inventory position and demand projections, we calculate the date when our stock will finsh up
    * We need to ensure that we inbound new stock 2 months before that date
    * Using Tranportation (Supplier to warehouse) lead times and Production lead times (PO creation to pickup), we estimate PO creation date
* PO confirmation, We align with the supplier on Qty, Price, Payment terms and Pickup date
* PI payment and production ready date collections, we request suppliers to share their production ready date to plan for future orders
* Production and PRD reconfirmation - Towards the end of the producion, we reconfirm the production ready so that we can book shipments
* Shipment batching - Based on confirmed PRD, 
    * we create shipment batches and initiate packing list collection
    * Initiate QC if required
    * Initiate commercial invoice colletion and payment
* Based on packing lists, we ask freight forwarders to reserve container space (LCL-FCL, and so on)


Impact of delays and 

* Any delay more than 15 days can lead to out of stock, and therefore revenue loss 
* If we go below our safety stock then we can't market our product
* If we go out of stock then there are amazon penalties 
* OOS also leads to lower product rank 


Impact of multiple changes changes in PRD

* Re-booking of vessels, re-booking of amazon warehouse inbound
* As freight volumes and prices are noegotiated based on monthly pickup projections, we have to pay extra in case of under-booking and over-booking in a month
* Lower supplier reliability and vendor score (High vendor score → More SKUs, POs
